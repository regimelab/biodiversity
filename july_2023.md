**July 2023** marked the month with the overall peak global sea surface temperature (SST) in 2023. It set the record for the highest monthly SST anomaly at +0.99°C (+1.78°F) above average in NOAA's 174-year record. [ncei.noaa](https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/access/monitoring/monthly-report/global/202307)

## Key Context
This peak followed record highs in June (+1.05°C anomaly) and preceded August's +1.03°C anomaly. July's extreme warmth aligned with El Niño onset, marine heatwaves across regions like the North Atlantic (1.05°C above average), and the fourth straight month of record ocean heat. [wmo](https://wmo.int/media/news/july-2023-confirmed-hottest-month-record)
